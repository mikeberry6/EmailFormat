Global Infrastructure M&A Activity
Infrastructure Coverage & Advisory Group February 7–13, 2026
 
Key Themes
Deal flow this week was headlined by Blackstone & EQT’s $6.6B joint acquisition of Urbaser, one of the largest environmental services transactions of 2026. Power & energy transition accounted for half the week’s activity, with CPP Investments’ $3.4B entry into Inkia Energy marking continued sovereign capital deployment into Latin American generation platforms. Digital infrastructure consolidation continued with InfraBridge’s fiber merger in the UK.
